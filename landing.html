About WardPulse AI

WardPulse AI is a next-generation civic complaint management platform that leverages artificial intelligence to streamline issue reporting, prioritization, and resolution across urban wards.

AI-Powered

Smart category detection, duplicate prevention, and priority classification powered by Gemini AI.

Geo-Tagged

Every issue is mapped with GPS coordinates. View all complaints on an interactive map.

Transparent

Real-time status updates, public voting, and full transparency in complaint resolution.
